# Code of Conduct

## Standard

This project expects respectful, technically honest collaboration.

Participants should:

- critique ideas directly without attacking people
- explain tradeoffs and constraints clearly
- avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and bad-faith conduct
- respect repository scope and maintainer decisions

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:

- personal attacks
- threats or harassment
- discriminatory language or conduct
- deliberately disruptive behavior
- publishing private information without permission

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and discussions that violate this policy or put the project at risk.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact path in [`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md) until a dedicated conduct address is published.
